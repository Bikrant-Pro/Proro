Sathya Sai Shiksha Sadan is an English medium school. It is a not-for-profit educational institution for students from Grades 1 - 12. Established in 1996 A.D. the school prescribes curriculum guided by the Government of Nepal and follows the National Examination Board. Over 1000 students have graduated with SEE/SLC Examinations from Year 2002 to 2023. The school provides a nurturing environment for youngsters both residential and non-residential and supports their holistic growth. Students are in the care of resident and non-resident teachers and staff in the tranquil and spacious premises of 33 Ropani. Two academic buildings, two separate hostels for boys and girls, two teachers' quarters, and an administrative block comprise our infrastructure where development of character and blossoming of human excellence is aspired.
