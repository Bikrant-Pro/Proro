Interactivity FAQs

Q: How can I interact with other hostelmates?

A: We encourage group activities, events, and social gatherings. You can also join study sessions or participate in organized tournaments!

Q: Are meals provided in the hostel?

A: Yes, the hostel offers breakfast, lunch, and dinner every day, with a variety of meal options catering to different tastes and dietary preferences.

Q: Is there Wi-Fi in the hostel?

A: Absolutely! We provide high-speed Wi-Fi access in all rooms and common areas to help you stay connected.
